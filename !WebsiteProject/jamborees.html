Jamborees

What is a Jamboree?

A Scout jamboree is a large event with many Scout groups. They vary a lot in size some are a few thousand, but some go above fifty thousand. Jamborees usually last between one and two weeks and are almost always during the summer but rarely some are during spring break.

World jamboree (WJ23/27)

World Jamborees are events where Scouts from all over the world get together to go to a huge camp. They happen every four years and every youth gets to go to one. The last one was called WJ23 and was in Saemangeum South Korea, it was a great learning experince and everyone who went will have memories of it for the rest of their lives. Pacific Jamboree (PJ)

The Pacific Jamboree is a Jamboree that takes place on the coast in BC usually on Vancouver island. Ventures can not go as participants but they can go as offer of service or OOS. They are basically volunteers and do work at the camps, you can work in first aid, security, or you can run activities.

International Jamborees

International Jamborees are like world Jamborees but a bit smaller and Ventures can go to multiple. There are multiple in different countries every year this year there is Norway, Portugal, and Australia. These Jamborees are filled with activities, and you usually get to travel around the country you are going to.
